Privacy Policy

SwingCoach · NordStudious · Last updated: April 2026

SwingCoach is a golf swing training app developed by NordStudious. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

SwingCoach uses your device's built-in motion sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope) to analyze your golf swing. This sensor data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

When you use the AI coaching feature, anonymized swing metrics (tempo ratio, rhythm score, transition smoothness, impact sharpness) are sent to our secure API for analysis. No personal information is included in these requests.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information. We do not use cookies or tracking technologies. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. AI Coaching

The AI coaching feature sends anonymized swing data to Anthropic's Claude API to generate feedback. This data contains only numerical metrics and no personal information. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing and can be found at anthropic.com/privacy.

4. Data Storage

SwingCoach stores your swing history locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app.

5. Permissions

SwingCoach requests access to your device's motion sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope) to detect and analyze your golf swing. This is the only permission required. We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

6. Children's Privacy

SwingCoach is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at nordstudious@gmail.com.
