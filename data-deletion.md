# Data Deletion Request – SwingCoach

**App name:** SwingCoach  
**Developer:** NordStudious (Sandnes Mekaniske)  
**Contact:** nordstudious@gmail.com

## How to request data deletion

To request deletion of your data associated with SwingCoach, send an email to:

**nordstudious@gmail.com**

With the subject line: **"Delete my SwingCoach data"**

Please include the email address or purchase receipt ID associated with your subscription (if applicable) so we can identify your account.

We will process your request within **30 days** and confirm the deletion by email.

## What data is deleted

Upon request, the following data will be deleted:

- Anonymous user ID associated with your device
- Subscription and purchase history stored by RevenueCat (our payment processor)
- Any swing data sent to our backend for AI coaching analysis

## What data is retained

Some data may be retained for legal and accounting purposes:

- **Transaction records** – retained for 5 years as required by Norwegian bookkeeping law (Bokføringsloven)
- **Tax documentation** – retained as required by Norwegian tax authorities

After the legal retention period, this data is also deleted.

## Local data on your device

Data stored locally on your device (swing history, app preferences) is not deleted automatically – you can remove it by uninstalling the app.
